How To Buy

STEP 1
Download Metamask

From The App Store or Google Play Store, for free. Or add the desktop browser extension on Google Chrome by going to metamask.io.

Follow their easy steps from their guide to set up a wallet.

STEP 2
Send AVAX to Metamask

You can buy AVAX directly through Metamask or you can transfer some from another wallet or crypto exchange.

STEP 3
Connect To Traderjoe

Go to Traderjoe.finance via a desktop browser or inside your Metamask wallet tap the three blue lines at the top left of your screen to reveal your browser.

When Metamask asks for your signature go ahead and click sign.

STEP 4
Swap your AVAX for $FAV

Click the cog button and change the Slippage amount to 8-12%. Add your desired AVAX amount you’d like to swap, once you’re happy click the “Swap” button to finalize the trade.
